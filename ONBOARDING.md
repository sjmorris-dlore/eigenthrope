# Eigenthrope Onboarding

*How to set up Xaman and claim your Observer identity.*

Eigenthrope does not use email, passwords, or platform accounts.

Your **Xaman wallet** is your Observer identity. It is how you connect to the story, sign your observations, build Resonance, and become part of the permanent record on the XRP Ledger.

The characters forget.

The players remember.

---

## What You Need

Before you begin, you will need:

- A mobile phone
- The Xaman app
- An XRPL account in Xaman
- At least enough XRP to activate the account and cover small XRPL transaction fees
- A safe offline place to write down any recovery information Xaman shows you

Xaman's official installation guide says setup usually takes about 10 minutes. Use the official guide for current device requirements and app store links:

- [Installing Xaman](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/installing-xumm)

---

## Step 1: Install Xaman

1. Open the official Xaman installation guide:
   - [Installing Xaman](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/installing-xumm)
2. Install Xaman from the iOS App Store or Google Play.
3. Open Xaman.
4. Set a 6-digit passcode.
5. Enable biometrics if you want to unlock Xaman or sign transactions with your device's biometric authentication.

For more detail on biometrics, use Xaman's guide:

- [How to configure biometrics](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/how-to-configure-biometrics)

---

## Step 2: Create or Import an XRPL Account

In Xaman, add an XRP Ledger account.

You can either:

- Create a new XRPL account, or
- Import an existing XRPL account you already control

If you create a new account, take your time with the recovery information. Xaman explains that account secrets are not displayed again after they are imported or created, so losing recovery information can mean losing access to the account.

Useful Xaman references:

- [Your first XRP Ledger account](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/your-first-xrp-ledger-account)
- [Importing your account (XRPL)](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/importing-your-account-xrpl)
- [Can I view/export my account secret?](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/can-i-view-export-my-account-secret)

## Step 3: Activate the Account

A new XRPL account must be activated before it can hold XRP or sign normal transactions.

Xaman's current help documentation says an XRPL account must be activated with a minimum of **1 XRP**. That first 1 XRP meets the XRP Ledger reserve requirement and is not spendable unless the account is later deleted and the reserve is moved elsewhere.

This is an XRP Ledger requirement, not an Eigenthrope fee. Eigenthrope does not receive the reserve and never controls your wallet.

Xaman lists several activation options, including buying XRP through Xaman partners, funding from another XRPL account in Xaman, or sending XRP from an external exchange or wallet.

Use Xaman's official guides for the current activation flow:

- [Activating an account (XRPL)](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/how-to-activate-a-new-xrpl-account)
- [Buying XRP](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/buying-xrp)
- [Sending XRP to Xaman](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/sending-xrp-to-xaman)

## Step 4: Connect to Eigenthrope

Once your Xaman wallet is installed and your XRPL account is active:

1. Visit Eigenthrope.
2. Press **Connect Xaman Wallet**.
3. Approve the connection in Xaman.
4. Return to Eigenthrope.

When the connection succeeds, Eigenthrope shows your shortened wallet address and your Observer profile.

This is not a username or password login. Your wallet address is your persistent Observer identity across universes.

---

## Step 5: Cast Your First Observation

Each open chapter presents one meaningful choice.

To vote:

1. Read the current chapter.
2. Review the options under **Your Observation**.
3. Choose the path you want the community to take.
4. When Eigenthrope shows a Xaman signing request, either:
   - Scan the QR code with Xaman, or
   - Tap **Open in Xaman** on your phone
5. Review the transaction in Xaman.
6. Sign it.
7. Return to Eigenthrope.

When the vote is accepted, Eigenthrope shows:

> Observation woven into the ledger.

That means your observation has been submitted as an XRP Ledger transaction. It becomes part of the public history of this universe.

For QR scanning help, use Xaman's guide:

- [How to scan a QR code](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/how-to-scan-a-qr-code)

## What Your Wallet Does in Eigenthrope

Your Xaman wallet gives you more than access to a vote button.

### Observer identity

Your wallet address is your persistent identity. When a universe collapses and the Eigenthropes forget, your participation history remains.

### Resonance

Each observation contributes to your Resonance. Resonance represents the strength of your presence as an Observer. Long-term participants gradually carry more influence because they have been part of more decisions.

### Public participation history

Every vote is recorded on the XRP Ledger with story-specific information such as the universe, chapter, choice point, choice, and vote weight.

### Discovery attribution

When the community uncovers a clue, the ledger can show which Observers participated in the decision that revealed it.

### Artifacts

Some choice points may later produce artifacts. If an artifact is available to you, Eigenthrope will show a **Claim Artifact** prompt. Claiming also happens through Xaman. Once claimed, the artifact lives in your wallet.

## Safety Notes

- Eigenthrope will never ask for your secret numbers, family seed, recovery phrase, or private key.
- Do not share your Xaman recovery information with anyone.
- Only sign transactions you understand.
- If a signing request looks unexpected, reject it and return to Eigenthrope.
- Use Xaman's official Help Center for wallet-specific support:
  - [Xaman Help Center](https://help.xaman.app/)

Eigenthrope can request signatures from your wallet. It cannot take your recovery information, cannot hold your keys, and cannot recover your wallet for you.

## Troubleshooting

### Xaman does not open

Make sure Xaman is installed on the phone you are using. If you are on desktop, use the QR code flow. If you are on mobile, use **Open in Xaman**.

### The QR code is not scanning

Open Xaman, tap the center scan icon, choose **Scan QR code**, and point your phone at the code.

Xaman reference:

- [How to scan a QR code](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/how-to-scan-a-qr-code)

### My account is not activated

New XRPL accounts need the activation reserve before they can be used normally. Follow Xaman's account activation guide:

- [Activating an account (XRPL)](https://help.xaman.app/app/getting-started-with-xaman/how-to-activate-a-new-xrpl-account)

### I signed in with the wrong wallet

Use **Disconnect** in Eigenthrope, then connect again with the Xaman account you want to use as your Observer identity.

### My vote expired or I rejected it

Return to Eigenthrope and choose again while voting is still open. A rejected or expired signing request does not count as a vote.

### I changed my mind

If voting is still open, Eigenthrope may let you change your observation. The latest valid signed vote for the choice point is the one that counts.
