# Eigenstate

A community-driven narrative game built on the XRP Ledger.

Every chapter presents a choice.

The community votes.

The story changes.

Eventually every universe ends.

The characters forget.

The players do not.

Across a growing chain of universes, the community accumulates knowledge that no character possesses, uncovering a mystery hidden beneath the worlds they help create.

Every vote is recorded on-chain.

Every universe becomes part of a permanent historical archive.

The real progression is not saving a universe.

It is understanding reality itself.

---

## The Premise

Eigenstate is a persistent, community-driven story in which players collectively guide the fate of recurring characters across multiple universes.

Each universe presents a narrative crisis.

Players vote on key decisions.

Those decisions shape relationships, reveal clues, and determine how the story unfolds.

Eventually the universe reaches a point where the Eigenthropes can no longer remain within it.

The story moves on.

The archive remains.

Future players can explore the full history of every universe, every decision, and every discovered clue.

Nothing is erased.

Everything matters.

---

## The Eigenthropes

At the center of the story are three recurring figures known as Eigenthropes.

They appear in different universes under different circumstances:

* Friends
* Enemies
* Strangers
* Allies
* Lovers
* Family

The same three people.

Infinite configurations.

They do not fully remember previous universes.

The community does.

That asymmetry is the foundation of the game.

## How It Works

### Read

A new chapter is released.

The community learns what is happening in the current universe and is presented with a meaningful choice.

### Vote

Players connect a wallet and vote through a transaction recorded on the XRP Ledger.

Every vote becomes part of the permanent historical record.

### Observe

The winning choice becomes reality.

The story advances.

Consequences unfold.

New information is revealed.

### Learn

Some choices reveal clues about a larger mystery that spans many universes.

Clues discovered in one universe may completely change how earlier universes are understood.

### Continue

Eventually the current universe ends.

A new universe begins.

The characters start over.

The players continue.

---

## Why XRPL?

Memory is one of the central themes of Eigenstate.

Universes end.

Characters forget.

The archive remains.

The XRP Ledger serves as an immutable record of player participation and community decisions, preserving the history of every universe long after its story concludes.

Rather than storing narrative state on-chain, Eigenstate uses XRPL as a transparent record of observation and choice.

The story itself evolves from that record.

---

## Community Progression

Players begin with equal influence.

Influence grows through participation, consistency, and engagement with the story.

This is intended to be a judgment economy rather than a popularity economy.

Raw social-media follower counts are excluded by design.

The goal is to reward participation, insight, and long-term involvement rather than external audience size.

---

## The Archive

Every universe becomes part of a permanent archive.

Players can review:

* Past universes
* Major decisions
* Community voting history
* Discovered clues
* Long-running mystery threads

The archive is not merely a record of completed content.

It is part of the game.

New players can study previous universes, build theories, and contribute to the community's growing understanding of the larger mystery.
