# Eigenthrope

*A persistent, collaborative narrative built on the XRP Ledger.*

Three beings exist who can slip between parallel universes.

They are called **Eigenthropes**.

Every universe eventually ends.

When it does, the Eigenthropes slip to a new eigenstate. Relationships reset. Memories vanish.

**The players remember.**

Across a growing chain of universes, the community accumulates knowledge that no character possesses, uncovering a mystery hidden beneath the worlds they help shape.

Every decision becomes part of the world's permanent history.

The story evolves.

The archive remains.

---

# What Is Eigenthrope?

Eigenthrope is a persistent collaborative narrative in which the community collectively determines how an evolving multiverse unfolds.

Each chapter presents a meaningful decision.

Players vote.

The outcome changes the current state of reality.

The next chapter is written from that new state.

Rather than following a fixed branching tree, the story evolves through the community's accumulated decisions, discoveries, and understanding.

---

# The Core Loop

1. Read the current chapter.
2. Vote on the next meaningful decision.
3. The community determines the outcome.
4. The world changes.
5. New clues emerge.
6. The story continues.
7. Eventually the current universe ends.
8. A new universe begins.
9. The community carries its knowledge forward.

The characters forget.

The players do not.

---

# A Persistent Mystery

Every universe tells its own complete story.

At the same time, every universe contributes to a much larger mystery spanning countless realities.

Community decisions determine:

* which questions get investigated,
* which clues are discovered,
* how mystery threads evolve,
* and what future generations of players will know.

The experience is intentionally archaeological.

The community doesn't simply solve puzzles.

It gradually discovers the shape of the mystery itself.

---

# The Archive

Every universe becomes part of a permanent archive.

Players can revisit:

* previous universes,
* major community decisions,
* discovered clues,
* evolving mystery threads,
* and the complete history of community participation.

The archive is not simply a record of completed content.

It is part of the game itself.

---

# Why XRPL?

Memory is one of Eigenthrope's central themes.

Universes end.

Characters forget.

The archive remains.

Every community vote is recorded as an XRP Ledger transaction containing the universe, chapter, choice point, and player influence.

The ledger becomes a permanent public history of every meaningful decision ever made.

Rather than storing gameplay state on-chain, Eigenthrope uses XRPL as an immutable record of observation and participation.

**The blockchain is not simply infrastructure. It is the story's memory.**

---

# Community Progression

Every player begins with equal influence.

Identity is established by connecting a Xaman wallet.

Influence is earned through participation rather than wealth, popularity, or social-media following.

Each vote contributes to a player's **Narrative Gravity**, allowing long-term participants to carry greater influence over future decisions.

This is intended to be a judgment economy, not a follower economy.

---

# State-Driven Storytelling

Eigenthrope is intentionally **not** authored as a traditional branching choose-your-own-adventure.

Instead, community decisions update the current state of reality.

Each new chapter is written from that state.

**The world is planned.**

**The mystery is planned.**

**The plot is discovered.**

This allows the narrative to remain responsive to the community without requiring an exponentially growing branch tree.
